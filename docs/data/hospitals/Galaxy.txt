Galaxy Hospital  is an excellent multi speciality center with outstanding state of the art facilities. Galaxy hospital has some of the best medical technology along with a team of doctors and support staff who are committed to provide a "patient first"  approach . 

Set up by Dr. Ravish kolwalkar who himself is well known for his prowess in the field of orthopedics, the hospital provides services in the field of Orthopedics, Cosmetology, Opthalmology, Minimal Access Surgery and more. In a short period of time, Galaxy hospital has been able to create a reputation for itself as one of the finest healthcare provider in the state of Goa
